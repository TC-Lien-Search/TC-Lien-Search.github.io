    Services

    Municipal Lien Search

    I reach out to the relevant municipality to search for unrecorded liens and code enforcement violations.

    Property Tax Search

    Included in your report is a search for property homestead exemption and a detailed history of property’s taxes.

    Permit Search

    The property’s detailed permit history is provided to uncover any open or expired permits.

    OFAC, Judgment and Federal searches for both the buyer and seller ensure a smooth transaction.
